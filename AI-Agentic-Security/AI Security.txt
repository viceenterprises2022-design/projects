## COMPETITIVE INTELLIGENCE — URL ANALYSIS

Product context: [PRODUCT_NAME] | [DOMAIN]

Competitor to analyze:
Name: [competitor name]
URL: [link]
Relationship: [direct / indirect / emerging threat / partial overlap]

Analyze and store competitive intelligence across:
1. Positioning — how do they describe themselves? who is their target user?
2. Feature set — what core capabilities do they offer?
3. Pricing model — tiers, free/paid, enterprise?
4. Differentiators — what do they claim to do uniquely?
5. Weaknesses / gaps — what are they NOT solving?
6. Signals — funding, team size, traction markers visible
7. Our edge — where does [PRODUCT_NAME] have an advantage over them?

Output as a competitor card I can add to my competitive map.

This week I fed you the following inputs (summarise what was shared):
- Articles: [titles or topics]
- Competitors analyzed: [names]
- Data files: [file names or datasets]
- Other inputs: [meetings, user calls, demos]

Synthesize across all stored context and produce:
1. Top 3 validated insights about our market or product
2. Top 3 open questions that research hasn't answered yet
3. Competitive landscape shift — any moves by competitors this week?
